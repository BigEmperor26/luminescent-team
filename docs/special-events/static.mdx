# Special Encounters
:::info
No Pokémon in Luminescent Platinum are shiny-locked. Knock out/run away from wild encounters to reset until shiny.
:::
<h3>Stitched Gengar</h3>
<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/TeamLumi/luminescent-team/main/static/img/pm0094_01_00_00_L.png" align="right" />Location: Old Chateau<br/>  
Level: 20<br />
<ul><li> Talk to the painting with the glowing red eyess in one of the rooms at the back of the Old Chateau.</li>
<li> Attempt to leave the room and you'll encounter a special, costumed Gengar.</li>
<li> This Gengar is mechanically identical to a regular Gengar.</li></ul><br />
<hr />

<h3>Spiritomb</h3>
<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/TeamLumi/luminescent-team/main/static/img/pm0442_00_00_00_L.png" align="right" />Location: Route 209<br />
Level: 30<br />
<ul><li> Get the Odd Keystone from a man on Route 208, hidden in Twinleaf Town or from the Underground.</li>
<li> Talk to the broken stone tower to encounter Spiritomb.</li></ul><br />
<hr />

<h3>Gabite</h3>
<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/TeamLumi/luminescent-team/main/static/img/pm0444_00_00_00_L.png" align="right" />Location: Wayward Cave<br />
Level: 25<br />
<ul><li> Go through the basement and up the stairs to the small room.</li>
<li> Walk forward and you'll encounter Gabite.</li></ul><br />
<hr />

<h3>Rotom</h3>
<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/TeamLumi/luminescent-team/main/static/img/pm0479_00_02_00_L.png" align="right" />Location: Old Chateau<br />
Level: 20<br />
<ul><li> Talk to the TV in one of the rooms at the back of the Old Chateau.</li>
<li> The event is now repeatable and can be done at any time of day.</li>
<ul><li> The key you earn can be used on Eterna's Galactic HQ first floor after defeating Jupiter.</li></ul></ul>
<hr />
	
<h3>Crystal Onix</h3>
<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/TeamLumi/luminescent-team/main/static/img/pm0095_01_00_00_L.png" align="right" />Location: Jubilife City<br />
Level: ??<br />
<ul><li> After defeating Wake (or on your way to Canalave), speak with the NPCs in the back of Jubilife Poke Mart.</li>
<li> Walk north of the city toward the Ravaged Path.</li>
<li> Head inside the Ravaged Path and turn left. Surf down the waterway until you reach land, and pass into the cave system while defeating Graveler in the way.</li>
<li> You will locate Crystal Onix in the very back.</li></ul>
<hr />
	
<h3>Clone Pokémon</h3>
Venusaur, Charizard, Blastoise, Pikachu<br />
Location: Oreburgh Gate (B1F)<br />
Level: ??<br />
<ul><li> During the course of [Mewtwo's event](legendaries.mdx#mewtwo), you will fight four Clone Pokemon.</li>
<li> You may fight them in any order and must catch them to proceed.</li></ul>
