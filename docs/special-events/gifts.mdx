# Gift Pokémon
:::info
No Pokémon in Luminescent Platinum are shiny-locked. Save before every gift to shiny hunt.
:::
<h3>Baby Pokémon Egg Gift</h3>
<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/TeamLumi/luminescent-team/main/static/img/000%20Base%20Egg.png" align="right" />Location: Jubilife City Trainer School<br />
Level: 1
<ul><li>Talk to the Cowgirl in the Jubilife School to receive an Egg.</li>
<li>The Egg will hatch into one of the following Pokémon at random:<br />
	Pichu, Cleffa, Igglybuff, Togepi, Tyrogue, Smoochum, Elekid, Magby, Azurill,<br />
	Wynaut, Budew, Chingling, Bonsly, Mime Jr., Happiny, Munchlax, Riolu, Mantyke.</li>
<li>You can save before collecting the Egg and soft reset if you don't like the result.</li></ul><br /><br />
<hr />

<h3>Cynthia Egg Gift</h3>
<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/TeamLumi/luminescent-team/main/static/img/000%20Base%20Egg.png" align="right" />Location: Eterna City<br />
Level: 1
<ul><li>After defeating Jupiter in Eterna Galactic HQ, Cynthia will approach and give you an Egg.</li>
<li>Previously, this Egg contained a Togepi. It is now randomized between: <br /> 
	Riolu, Budew, Happiny, Togepi.</li>
<li>If you obtained any of these babies from the Jubilife Baby Egg, you will <i>not</i> get them again here.</li></ul><br /><br /><br />
<hr />

<h3>Kanto Starters</h3>
Bulbasaur, Charmander, Squirtle<br />
Location: Jubilife City Pokémon Center<br />
Level: 5 
<ul><li>Talk to the Interviewer at the top left.</li>
<li>Answer her questions correctly and win the battle.</li>
<li>You may choose the order between Bulbasaur, Charmander and Squirtle, and can collect all three.</li>
<li>If your party gets full before all three are given, she will send them to your box.</li></ul>
<hr />

<h3>Sinnoh Starters</h3>
Turtwig, Chimchar, Piplup<br />
Location: Sandgem Town<br />
Level: 5
<ul><li>Interact with the briefcase at the top right of Rowan's lab.</li>
<li>Rowan will say a few words and then allow you to take out the Poké Balls inside.</li>
<li>Interact with the briefcase again to receive the two Sinnoh starters that you didn't choose.</li>
<li>Turtwig, Chimchar and Piplup will be given in that order, with the one you already selected not appearing.</li></ul>
