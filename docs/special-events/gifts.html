Gift Pokémon

info

No Pokémon in Luminescent Platinum are shiny-locked. Save before every gift to shiny hunt.

PokémonInfo

Baby Pokémon Egg

Level 1
Jubilife Trainer School
  • Talk to the Cowgirl in the Jubilife School to receive an Egg.
  • The Egg will hatch into one of the following Pokémon at random:
    Pichu, Cleffa, Igglybuff, Togepi, Tyrogue, Smoochum, Elekid, Magby, Azurill,
    Wynaut, Budew, Chingling, Bonsly, Mime Jr., Happiny, Munchlax, Riolu, Mantyke.
  • You can save before collecting the Egg and soft reset if you don't like the result.

Cynthia Egg Gift

Level 1
Eterna City
  • After defeating Jupiter in Eterna Galactic HQ, Cynthia will approach and give you an Egg.
  • Previously, this Egg contained a Togepi. It is now randomized between:
    Riolu, Budew, Happiny, Togepi.
  • You can save before collecting the Egg and soft reset if you don't like the result.
  • If you obtained any of these babies from the Jubilife Baby Egg, you will not get them again here.

Kanto Starters

Bulbasaur, Charmander, Squirtle

Level 5
Jubilife City Pokémon Center
  • Talk to the Interviewer at the top left.
  • Answer her questions correctly and win the battle.
  • You may choose the order between Bulbasaur, Charmander and Squirtle, and can collect all three.
  • If your party gets full before all three are given, she will send them to your box.

Lapras

Level 35
Pastoria City
  • In one of the houses is an old lady. Agree to listen to her story.
  • She'll give you a Lapras after she finishes.

Ditto

Level 30
Solaceon Town
  • In the Nursery, speak to the man inside.
  • You will receive a Ditto with maximum IVs in every stat that is holding a Destiny Knot.
  • Happy breeding.

Eevee

Level 5
Twinleaf Town
  • Complete the events as far as Rowan's Assistant telling you to speak to your mother.
  • Speak to your mother and she'll suggest taking Eevee.
  • Interact with the Eevee to receive it.

Porygon

Level 22
Eterna City Galactic Building
  • Defeat Jupiter at the Eterna Galactic Building.
  • Interact with the Poké Ball behind her to get Porygon.

Fossils

Omanyte, Kabuto, Aerodactyl, Lileep, Anorith, Cranidos, Shieldon
Level 20
Eterna City
  • Go to the Underground Man's house, near the Pokémon Center in Eterna City.
  • Talk to the female scientist to receive fossils for all of these Pokémon.
  • Take the fossils to the museum in Oreburgh City to revive them.

Johto Starters

Chikorita, Cyndaquil, Totodile

Level 15
Floaroma Town Pokémon Center
  • Talk to the Interviewer at the top left.
  • Answer her questions correctly and win the battle.
  • You may choose the order between Chikorita, Cyndaquil and Totodile, and can collect all three.
  • If your party gets full before all three are given, she will send them to your box.

Spiky-eared Pichu

Level 30
Celestic Town
  • Go to Celestic Town with a Pichu in your party.
  • If shiny-hunting, save before continuing.
  • Interact with the shrine to obtain a special Pichu.

Hoenn Starters

Treecko, Torchic, Mudkip

Level 10
Oreburgh City Pokémon Center
  • Talk to the Interviewer at the top left.
  • Answer her questions correctly and win the battle.
  • You may choose the order between Treecko, Torchic and Mudkip, and can collect all three.
  • If your party gets full before all three are given, she will send them to your box.

Beldum

Level 10
Oreburgh City
  • Go inside the house near Oreburgh Mine.
  • Talk to Steven and he'll offer you a Beldum.

Sinnoh Starters

Turtwig, Chimchar, Piplup

Level 5
Sandgem Town
  • Interact with the briefcase at the top right of Rowan's lab.
  • Rowan will say a few words and then allow you to take out the Poké Balls inside.
  • Interact with the briefcase again to receive the two Sinnoh starters that you didn't choose.
  • Turtwig, Chimchar and Piplup will be given in that order, with the one you already selected not appearing.
