# Special Encounters
:::info
No Pokémon in Luminescent Platinum are shiny-locked. Knock out/run away from wild encounters to reset until shiny.
:::
### Stitched Gengar
---
<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/TeamLumi/luminescent-team/main/static/img/pm0094_01_00_00_L.png" align="right" />Location: Old Chateau  
Level: 20
- Talk to the painting with the glowing red eyess in one of the rooms at the back of the Old Chateau.
- Attempt to leave the room and you'll encounter a special, costumed Gengar.
- This event can only be performed once.
- This Gengar is mechanically identical to a regular Gengar.

### Spiritomb
---
<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/TeamLumi/luminescent-team/main/static/img/pm0442_00_00_00_L.png" align="right" />Location: Route 209  
Level: 30
- Get the Odd Keystone from a man on Route 208, hidden in Twinleaf Town or from the Underground.
- Talk to the broken stone tower to encounter Spiritomb.

### Gabite
---
<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/TeamLumi/luminescent-team/main/static/img/pm0444_00_00_00_L.png" align="right" />Location: Wayward Cave  
Level: 25
- Go through the basement and up the stairs to the small room.
- Walk forward and you'll encounter Gabite.
- This event can only be performed once.
<br /><br />

### Rotom
---
<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/TeamLumi/luminescent-team/main/static/img/pm0479_00_02_00_L.png" align="right" />Location: Old Chateau  
Level: 20
- Talk to the TV in one of the rooms at the back of the Old Chateau.
- The event is now repeatable and can be done at any time of day.
	- The key you earn can be used on Eterna's Galactic HQ first floor after defeating Jupiter.
<br /><br /><br /><br /><br />


	
### Crystal Onix
--- 
<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/TeamLumi/luminescent-team/main/static/img/pm0095_01_00_00_L.png" align="right" />Location: Jubilife City  
Level: ??
- After defeating Wake (or on your way to Canalave), speak with the NPCs in the back of Jubilife Poke Mart.
- Walk north of the city toward the Ravaged Path.
- Head inside the Ravaged Path and turn left. Surf down the waterway until you reach land, and pass into the cave system while defeating Graveler in the way.
- You will locate Crystal Onix in the very back.
- This event can only be performed once.

Venusaur, Charizard, Blastoise, Pikachu

Level: ??
