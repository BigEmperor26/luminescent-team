      <td><ul>
        <li>Can be found outside Jubilife Trainer School.</li>
        <li>In exchange for one bottlecap, she can raise a Pokémon's IV to 31.</li>

        <li>In exchange for one golden bottle cap, she can raise all six of a Pokémon's IVs to 31.</li>
